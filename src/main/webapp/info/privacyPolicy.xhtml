Privacy Policy

bjmanch.org operates the site (www.bjmanch.org) to provide services including social media.

bjmanch.org is committed to protecting users' privacy and addressing potential privacy concerns. The following information has been posted to help you, the consumer, understand what private information we collect and how your information is used.

Types of private information collected

To best serve your needs, we collect the following types of information when you visit (website).

Personal information

We collect the following personal information from our users:

  • Personal Details: Firstname, Last Name, Email address, gender, date of birth
  • Political Constituency: Lok Sabha Constituency, Vidhan Sabha Constituency, State/UT name

Automatically collected information

We automatically collect data on how our users visit www.bjmanch.org,, such as your IP address, location, browser, browser language, operating system and device identifiers, and cookies.

We do not link your account with any social media account. But this policy might change in the future. If that happens we will change this Privacy Policy accordingly.

Policy on collecting sensitive personal information

www.bjmanch.org does not gather any of your sensitive personal information, such as your Aadhar Card Number, Passport number, driver's license number, race, ethnicity, religion, health metrics, political associations, or details on your criminal background.How the information collected is used We only use your personal information to help you utilize everything has to offer. This includes:

  • Creating and maintaining an account with us
  • Creating Forums, Surveys and Blogs
  • Participating in all Forums, Surveys and Blogs
  • ‘Report Abuse’ functionality provided with Forums, Surveys and Blogs.
  • At the time of nominating a Candidate for Lok Sabha elections from your Lok Sabha constituency.
  • At the time of nominating a Candidate for Vidhan Sabha elections from your Vidhan Sabha constituency.
  • At times when we send you emails in the following scenarios:
    • Successful creation of a Forum or a Survey.
    • Successful publishing of a Blog.
    • Lok Sabha candidate nomination.
    • Vidhan Sabha candidate nomination.
    • When you report abuse on the Forum, Survey or Blog published by you.
    • When some other user of www.bjmanch.org contacts us to report any abuse you have posted while posting comments on their Forum(s), Survey(s) or Blog(s).

Length of time personal information is stored

We may store your personal information for an extended period of time, depending on how we need to use the information. For example, we'll keep your email address and name on file as long as

  • you have comments posted on Forums, Surveys and Blogs.
  • You have nominated a candidate for your Lok Sabha and Vidhan Sabha constituencies - until the respective assemblies are dissolved for the next round of elections.

Who the information is shared with

We may share your information only with the government agencies if ever a need arises that the Government has valid reasons to check our users database. Other than that we might share your personal information with other web applications that we launch in the future, but you would be duly informed in advance and final decision to avail our new services from our web sites under the same umbrella company. Moreover, it is obvious that as and when we registered an umbrella company, there would be an overhaul of the Privacy Policy for the umbrella company and all other websites that would be included within the folds off the umbrella company, including the existing website www.bjmanch.org

Information Security

We do our best to keep your private information secure and safe. Most of your personal information is encrypted and is therefore protected from being accessed by outside users. However, data leaks and malicious attacks do happen. Please understand that while we do our best to protect your data, providing your personal information is at your own risk.

Ability to decline to provide personal information

You can, of course, decline to disclose your personal information to us. However, the failure to provide us with pertinent information may limit your ability to fully benefit from and all of its offerings. Remember, we only ask for personal information that can help us fully provide our services to our customers.

Additional questions

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, feel free to contact us at contact@bjmanch.org . A bjmanch.org representative will respond to your privacy policy questions as soon as possible.

Policy update

This privacy policy was last updated on 3rd January, 2025. If we adjust the privacy policy, we will email all account holders of this change and post the most recent update date here. Once posted, the new privacy policy shall be effective immediately.
